Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Safe Women's surveillance system

How does the assault detection system work?

Our system uses advanced YOLO (You Only Look Once) AI technology to analyze video feeds in real-time. It's trained to recognize patterns and movements associated with assault situations, triggering immediate alerts when suspicious activity is detected.

What types of cameras are compatible?

Our system works with most modern IP cameras and webcams that can provide an RTSP or HTTP stream. This includes popular brands like Hikvision, Dahua, and standard USB webcams.

How are emergency contacts notified?

When an incident is detected, the system automatically sends SMS alerts to your verified emergency contacts, including the location and timestamp of the incident. Contacts must be verified through our system before receiving alerts.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. All video feeds are encrypted, and we follow strict data protection protocols. Your personal information and emergency contact details are stored securely and never shared with third parties.
